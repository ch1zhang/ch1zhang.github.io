Chi Zhang is a Postdoctoral Researcher in the [Software System Security Assurance Group](http://www.wingtecher.com/homeen) at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), supervised by Associate Professor [Yu Jiang](https://sites.google.com/site/jiangyu198964/home). He received his Ph.D. in 2025 from the Software Engineering Group at Nanjing University, under the supervision of Professor Linzhang Wang. In 2018, he earned dual bachelorâ€™s degrees in Computer Science and Economics from [Sichuan University](https://en.scu.edu.cn/). From August 2022 to August 2023, he conducted research at the [Trustworthy Engineering of Software Technologies (TEST) Lab](https://nus-test.github.io/) at the National University of Singapore, supervised by [Manuel Rigger](https://www.manuelrigger.at/).


His research interests lie at the intersection of software engineering and machine learning, with a focus on static analysis, fuzzing, and integrating artificial intelligence to enhance software engineering practices. His work emphasizes detecting software bugs, and he has successfully identified nearly 80 bugs in mainstream database management systems.
